Future of Cloud Computing

"The Future of Cloud Computing" was successfuly held at NSBM School Of Computing,Nugegoda By Janaka Rangama of Virtusa, organized by NSBM DevOps Commiunity.

GDG Sri Lanka workshop on Google Cloud Platform

GDG Sri Lanka joined NSBM for a workshop on #Google #CloudPlatform and Introduction to #MachineLearning on 24th June from 9 AM onward. The event took place at NSBM School of Computing. It was discussed how #AI works, and more information on how to use #Google Cloud to make our life easy.

Hybrid Mobile Application Development Workshop

The Senior Developer at 99XTechnology, Hybrid Mobile Developer, UX Evangelist and the Co-founder of YEETUP.COM Mr. Kosala Nuwan Perera started up with the introduction of the wide ranged topic and the stack behind hybrid. Furthermore briefly explained about lean UX to design winning apps and how to fake it, make it and ship it. The participants were guided by Mr. Suranga Bandara, the Tech Lead at “99XTechnology”, Senior Developer, Scrum-master and Mr. Rashmika Nawaratne, Executive Director (CEO) at SL2COLLEGE, Software Engineer at 99X Technology and Hybrid Mobile Developer, to build a code-camp mobile app as a hands on training for the undergraduates regarding Hybrid Mobile Development.
